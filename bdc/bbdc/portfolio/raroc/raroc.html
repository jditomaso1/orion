Orion RAROC screening methodology
A transparent proxy designed for portfolio comparison, not an official BBDC calculation
Estimated risk score (1–5)Combines lien priority, fair-value-to-cost markdown, PIK exposure, maturity proximity and non-accrual status.
Expected credit lossEstimated probability of default by risk score × loss severity by seniority.
RAROC proxy(Reported all-in rate − expected credit loss) ÷ estimated economic-capital requirement.
Important limitationRisk scores, expected losses and capital requirements are Orion estimates derived from public position data. They are not BBDC internal risk ratings, regulatory capital measures, investment recommendations or audited RAROC figures. Equity and instruments without a reported all-in rate are excluded from return calculations.
