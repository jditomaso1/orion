How to interpret the watchlist

Direction evaluates the borrower-level trend, while the action reflects the risk to BBDC's specific instrument. A borrower can show deteriorating indicators while a small, senior and well-collateralized loan remains a maintain.

Coverage limitation

This comparison covers the three BBDC borrowers with sufficient public reporting for independent underwriting. Other disclosed BBDC positions remain available for mark, PIK, non-accrual and concentration screening but are not assigned borrower-level conclusions without underlying financial information.
