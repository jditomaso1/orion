Orion Credit View

BUY — Risk-Adjusted Return Attractive

At current levels (~85), the first-lien debt in Dave & Buster’s offers an attractive yield relative to underlying cash generation, leverage, and liquidity. While the business is exposed to discretionary consumer cycles and wage inflation, current pricing appears to over-discount these risks. Refinancing is manageable under base-case assumptions, and free cash flow provides a modest de-leveraging path.
