Orion Credit View

BUY — Defensive Aftermarket Profile, Manageable Leverage

First Brands benefits from a resilient, replacement-driven demand base, diversified brand portfolio and meaningful scale advantages. While leverage is elevated and customer concentration remains a key risk, margins and free cash flow are supported by leading market positions and broad category exposure. At current pricing, first-lien secured debt appears attractive on a risk-adjusted basis, assuming stable U.S. light vehicle miles driven and continued access to refinancing markets.

Watch Signals

Aftermarket demand

Replacement demand remains steady; monitor miles driven and scrappage trends for early inflection.

Cost inflation & pricing

Raw material and freight costs need to be passed through via pricing; watch gross margin stability.

Refinancing window

Term loan and notes maturities in 20XX–20YY require continued market access; spreads and auto-aftermarket sentiment should be monitored closely.
